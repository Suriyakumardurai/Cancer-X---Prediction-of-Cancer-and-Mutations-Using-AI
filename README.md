## 🚀 What is CancerX?

> **CancerX** is not just another AI model — it’s a bold **multimodal AI prototype** designed to **mimic expert clinicians**, synthesizing diverse medical signals to predict cancer types and mutations with **clinical-grade precision**.

### 🤯 Core Concept
We integrate **four pillars** of cancer diagnostics:

- 🩺 **Radiological Imaging** — CT, MRI, PET: macro-level tumor detection  
- 🔬 **Histopathology Slides** — WSIs: cellular-level insights  
- 📝 **Clinical Reports** — Radiology & pathology text: contextual understanding  
- 🧬 **Omics Data** — Genomics, proteomics, transcriptomics: deep molecular signals  

> 🧠 Our philosophy: **“No single test tells the whole story. Neither should AI.”**

---

## 🌌 Why Multimodal Matters

Predictive oncology is like solving a puzzle. Each modality is a piece — **CancerX brings them together** using advanced **fusion strategies** and **AI architectures**:

### 💡 Highlights
- 🧩 **Multimodal Fusion** — Early, Intermediate (SoTA), Late  
- 🤖 **Transformer-Powered** — ViT, BERT, CNN, hybrid GNNs  
- 📊 **Interactive Dashboards** — Transparent metrics, insightful comparisons  
- 🧭 **Clinical Translation Pathway** — Towards robustness, generalizability & real-world deployment
